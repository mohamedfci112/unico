The requirements of Unico NORM basics for standard safety rules, references & regulations were established in 1/1/2017 to be an effective step to Unico's History of basic safety standards for the protection of the health of workers and the general public.

Unico avoids any dangers arising from ionizing and ensures the health protection of all individuals against the dangers of radiation of NORM in relation to medical exposure and the requirements of Egyptian Atomic Energy Agency Safety Series and ENRRA Reference Rules.
