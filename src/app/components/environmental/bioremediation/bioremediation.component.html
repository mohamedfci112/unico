BIO-REMEDIATION

As a leading company in the Environmental Sector in the region; Unico’s role towards the increase of Oil Base Mud Cutting was find a solution to improve the efficiency of our treatment. For the first time in Egypt, Unico Introduced the latest technology to degrade the organic pollutant in Oil Base Mud Cutting; known as Bio-Remediation.

Accompanied by very minimal, negligible emissions to abide by the EEAA limits. This technology was researched and implemented by Unico’s R&D team and the Micro Biology professors at Alexandria University. A branch of biotechnology utilizes the biological mechanisms inherent in bacteria to eliminate the environmental contaminants and restore the ecosystem to its original condition.
